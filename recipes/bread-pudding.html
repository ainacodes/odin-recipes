Bread Pudding

This recipe is from Che Nom

Preparation time: 30 minutes

Cooking time: 45 - 50 minutes

Serving size: 9 x 9 inch pan

Ingredients

Bread pudding:

  • 8 slices of bread
  • 4 tablespoons of butter
  • ¼ cup of raisins
  • 2 cups of full cream milk
  • ½ cup of sugar
  • 1 teaspoon of vanilla extract
  • ¼ teaspoon of ground cinnamon
  • A pinch of salt
  • 3 eggs
  • Brown sugar, as needed

Custard Sauce:

  • 1 can of evaporated milk
  • 2 tablespoons of custard powder
  • 1 cup of water
  • 4 tablespoons of sugar
  • A pinch of salt

Instructions

Bread Pudding:

  1. Cut the bread into 8 pieces.
  2. Spread or grease a baking dish with butter.
  3. Arrange the bread pieces in the baking dish.
  4. In another bowl, combine full cream milk with sugar, vanilla extract, ground cinnamon, a pinch of salt, and eggs. Mix all the ingredients together.
  5. Melt the butter until it's liquid, then pour it over the bread.
  6. Sprinkle raisins and pour the milk mixture over the bread. Press the bread down to ensure it absorbs the mixture.
  7. Let it rest for about 5-10 minutes.
  8. Sprinkle a sufficient amount of brown sugar over the bread mixture, then bake at 170°C for 25-30 minutes until the bread pudding is cooked and the surface turns golden brown.

Custard Sauce:

  1. Mix evaporated milk, sugar, custard powder, and a pinch of salt. Stir until everything is fully dissolved.
  2. Once dissolved, add water and heat the custard sauce mixture. Cook until the custard sauce thickens evenly.

Bread Pudding & Custard Sauce:

  1. After baking, brush the surface of the still-warm bread with melted butter.
  2. Cut the bread pudding and serve it with custard sauce.

Tips

  • In addition to topping the bread pudding with raisins, you can also add chocolate chips or nuts such as almonds and hazelnuts.
  • To ensure that the custard sauce is cooked and thickened, you can do a finger swipe test on the back of a spoon. A properly thickened sauce will leave a mark on the spoon compared to a runny custard sauce.
